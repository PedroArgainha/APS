Frequently Asked Questions

What are the typical interest rates?

addremove

Interest rates vary depending on the loan type, your Crédito history, and the loan term. We pride ourselves on offering competitive rates and will provide a clear, detailed breakdown during your consultation.

What documents are required to apply?

addremove

Generally, you will need proof of identity, proof of income (such as payslips or bank statements), and details about your current financial situation. Specific requirements may vary by loan type.

How long does the approval process take?

addremove

Our goal is to provide a decision as quickly as possible. For most personal loans, approval can happen within 24-48 hours, while housing and business loans may take longer due to their complexity.
